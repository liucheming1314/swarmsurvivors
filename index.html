About the Game

Swarm Survivors is a fast, lightweight action roguelike designed for instant play. Every run drops you into a minimalist arena where monsters scale in numbers and pace. Your goal: kite efficiently, avoid contact, gather resources, and invest in upgrades that snowball your survivability.

How to Play

1) Keep Moving

Kite swarms in wide arcs. Staying mobile reduces contact damage and buys time for cooldowns.

2) Collect & Upgrade

Pick up orbs to unlock upgrades — prioritize area control, sustained DPS, and survivability.

3) Extend the Run

Stack synergies that scale over time, then push for your personal best score.
